Information page Route 66

Drag map/marker: Left click map/marker and drag mouse.
Add marker: Left click on map.
Delete marker: Right click marker or press Del.
Add Change marker: Left click marker or press C.
Add Navigation marker: Ctrl + left click marker or press N.
Select marker: Left click marker or hover over marker in expert mode.
Insert route: Select marker, then add marker or add route from menu.
Move current marker: Up/Down arrow.
Copy marker: Ctrl-X, Ctrl-V or just press X,V.
Animate current marker: Press and hold Up/Down arrow.
