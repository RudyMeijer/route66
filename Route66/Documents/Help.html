Information page Route 66

Drag map: Right click
Add marker: Left click.
Delete marker: Right click or press Del.
Add Change marker: Double click marker or press C.
Add Navigation marker: Ctrl + double click marker or press N.
Select marker: Left click marker. Hover over marker (In expert mode).
Insert route: Select marker, point empty location and add marker.
Move current marker: Up/Down arrow.
Animate current marker: Ctrl + Up/Down arrow.
